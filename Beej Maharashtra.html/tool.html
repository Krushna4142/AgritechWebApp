TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT FOR LAND DEVELOPMENT, TILLAGE AND SEEDBED PREPARATION

Land development is defined as removal of unwanted vegetation, clearing, and leveling with deep tillage operations, moving soil from high to low spots.

The tillage operations defined as mechanical manipulation of soil are performed to achieve favorable soil physical conditions required for seed germination and plant growth.
