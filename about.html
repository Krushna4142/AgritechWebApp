About Agritech – Intelligent Agriculture System

Agritech is a state-of-the-art intelligent agriculture platform meticulously designed and developed by Krushna Nawale, a Computer Engineering student at KIT. This system serves as a comprehensive solution to modern farming challenges by offering smart, data-driven tools for optimizing agricultural practices.

The platform offers AI-powered fertilizer recommendations, crop health monitoring, and weather-based farming advice. Through intelligent algorithms and an easy-to-use interface, Agritech enables farmers to increase productivity and achieve sustainable farming.

Developed as a Engineering project, the system demonstrates a professional approach by combining frontend technologies and intelligent backend modules for real-world usability.
